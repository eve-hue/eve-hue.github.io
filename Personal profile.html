Through these tests, I can understand my personality and find my strengths and weaknesses. For example, through the learning style test, I found that I might be better at auditory learning, so I think I will play a listener role in the team, so as to carry out the most efficient team cooperation. In the creativity test, I find that I may be too rigid in some directions and fields and lack enough innovation. Therefore, in the process of learning, I may pay more attention to the cultivation of my own creativity and try my best to innovate and design projects.

Considerations in team forming

When I am forming a team, I may use these test results as a part of the basis for my selection of members. Even if these data may deviate from the reality, these results will provide me with other people's personalities and abilities, which will help me find the members I need. I can quickly recognize whether other people are good at what the team needs to do and whether their personalities are suitable for the team. When the right people work together, the efficiency of the team will be improved.
